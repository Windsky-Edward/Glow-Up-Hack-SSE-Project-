Executive Summary

A smarter decision layer for beauty discovery.

The platform combines beauty profiling, personalised roadmaps, provider verification, booking requests and post-service guidance into one mobile-first marketplace experience.

For users

Clearer recommendations based on their actual needs, skin context, budget, style and comfort level.

For providers

Higher-intent clients matched to real expertise, not only advertising strength or social media reach.

For the market

More transparent comparison, less trial-and-error consumption and better trust signals for beauty decisions.

Market Problem

Beauty choices are emotional, expensive and hard to verify.

Users often make decisions from generic advice, filtered social content and incomplete provider information. That creates avoidable cost, disappointment and physical risk.

The trust gap

Users cannot easily verify whether online reviews, influencer claims, provider photos or treatment results are real.

01

Generic recommendations

Most advice does not fit different skin types, budgets, personal styles or comfort levels.

02

Wrong choices are costly

Beauty mistakes can waste money, consume time and create unnecessary physical risk.

03

Quality is hard to surface

Skilled providers may lose customers to competitors with stronger advertising rather than better service quality.

User Interview Insights

People want confidence before they commit.

Needs-led guidance

Users want recommendations based on their actual needs, not the most expensive service.

Verified evidence

Users care about verified results because social media can be misleading.

Sensitive-skin safety

Sensitive-skin users need safer guidance, clearer evidence and realistic expectation setting.

Provider intent

Providers want high-intent clients matched to their real expertise.

Target Market

Beauty-conscious Gen Z and millennial users in Kuala Lumpur and Klang Valley.

The initial market focuses on mobile-native users who already compare beauty content online but need a more trusted path to action.

Skincare guidance

Personalised routines, ingredient caution, skin goal planning and safer comparison.

Nail services

Style matching, provider portfolio review, pricing clarity and repeat booking support.

Selected non-invasive services

Risk-aware education, expectation setting and post-service guidance without medical diagnosis claims.

Our Solution

From profile to provider match to aftercare support.

Glow Up Hack helps users create a beauty profile, receive a personalised roadmap, compare verified providers, book suitable services and receive post-service support.

Create beauty profile

Capture skin condition, facial features, personal style, budget, comfort level and goals.

Personalised roadmap

Translate goals into practical, phased beauty actions that can evolve over time.

Compare verified providers

Show provider fit, portfolio signals, service categories and trust indicators.

Post-service guidance

Offer checklists, reminders and escalation suggestions when professional help may be needed.

Five AI Innovations

Purpose-built AI for safer beauty decisions.

DNA

Aesthetic DNA Engine

Creates a unique profile based on skin condition, facial features, style preferences, budget, comfort level and beauty goals.

MAP

Dynamic Glow-Up Roadmap

Builds a personalised beauty plan that evolves based on user feedback, environmental factors, lifestyle and progress.

FIT

AI-Audited Matching

Uses provider verification and portfolio checking to match users with suitable professionals based on real expertise.

SIM

Predictive Outcome Simulation

Helps users understand possible results before deciding, reducing uncertainty and unrealistic expectations.

24

24/7 AI Recovery Companion

Provides general post-service guidance, checklists, reminders and escalation suggestions when professional help may be needed.

WHY

Explainable recommendations

Shows users why a service, provider or product direction fits their profile, instead of hiding the logic.

Competitive Advantage

More than a booking app. More trusted than a social feed.

Compared with booking apps and social media, Glow Up Hack focuses on intelligent decision support, provider verification, risk-aware matching, personalised roadmaps and continued aftercare support.

Decision intelligence

Guides users before the booking instead of only listing available services.

Risk-aware matching

Uses user context and provider evidence to reduce mismatch and unrealistic expectations.

Continuous support

Extends beyond the transaction with roadmap updates and aftercare guidance.

MVP Scope

A focused prototype that proves the core loop.

The MVP should demonstrate account creation, beauty profile, personalised recommendations, provider comparison, booking request and post-service guidance. It should not claim medical diagnosis.

User flow

Account creation, guided assessment, Aesthetic DNA profile and personalised roadmap.

Marketplace flow

Provider comparison, service detail pages, trust indicators and booking request.

Support flow

General post-service guidance, reminders, feedback capture and escalation suggestions.

Safety, Privacy and Responsible AI

Trust must be designed into the product from day one.

Data and privacy

  • Consent-based data collection
  • Minimal data retention
  • Secure storage
  • Clear deletion controls

Responsible AI guardrails

  • Explainable recommendations
  • No absolute outcome promises
  • No medical diagnosis claims
  • Escalation to qualified professionals for serious concerns
